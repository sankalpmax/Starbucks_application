50 Years of serving communities

As we celebrate 50 years of serving coffee and community, we reflect on the journey that has brought us to this significant milestone. From humble beginnings in Seattle's Pike Place Market to becoming a global icon of coffee culture, Starbucks has always been about more than just coffee – it's about connection, community, and creating moments of joy.

Over the past five decades, we've grown from a single store to thousands of locations around the world, each one a beacon of warmth and familiarity for our customers. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation has remained steadfast, driving us to continuously raise the bar and redefine what it means to be a coffee company.
